# 🔍 Key Patterns From Inspo (Extracted)

### What works extremely well:

* **Strong above-the-fold clarity** (clear outcome + CTA)
* **Heavy social proof early** (logos, numbers, ratings)
* **Visual + human-first branding** (face = trust)
* **Modular card-based sections** (easy scanning)
* **Community + tools positioning** (not just “course”)
* **Dark + premium gradients for authority**
* **Repetition of CTA across scroll**

### Gaps you can improve:

* Less clutter → more **premium whitespace**
* Stronger **personal authority storytelling** (this is your edge)
* Clearer **framework positioning** (ownable IP)
